About Us

The Art of Grooming & Wellness

Located in the prestigious Gigiri Box Park at United Nations Crescent, Yankee Clippers represents the pinnacle of men and women grooming and wellness in Nairobi. We blend traditional barbering techniques with modern luxury spa treatments.

Our team of expert barbers and therapists are dedicated to providing you with an exceptional experience, whether you're preparing for a diplomatic meeting at the UN or simply treating yourself to premium self-care.
